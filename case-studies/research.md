# Research and Optimization

## Research themes

- Computer vision and biometric verification
- OCR and financial-document understanding
- AI software and inference optimization
- Quantum circuit optimization using geometric programming

## Research practice

I work across problem formulation, literature review, experiment design, implementation, evaluation, and technical writing. Selected manuscripts are in preparation or in the publication process; titles and artifacts can be shared when appropriate.

## Engineering standard

Research work is documented with assumptions, baselines, metrics, limitations, and reproducibility notes. I avoid presenting an internal result as a universal claim and distinguish reported project benchmarks from independently reproduced results.
